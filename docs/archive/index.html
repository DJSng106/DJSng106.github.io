The Archives

Well, hello! Welcome to the archives, a place I like to call the "back" of the website, like the warehouses in stores. This page is mostly just plain old HTML because I don't care much about presentation. The archives range in varying qualities, so instead of spending time fancifying a homepage, I would just rather, well... Write the damn thing. Some of these pages are coded like shit, why should I overly present them like they're peak? They're not. They're old drafts.

Everything you see here is mostly untampered aside from some changes. Some of those changes included manually fixed images (that is not 1:1 with the original code... who cares about that?), my old button replaced with my newest one (for privacy), some blanked out pages on irrelevant links, etc. A lot of stuff in the archives rot, so I tried my best to make them browsable so I could open them up to the public, for all to see what I made in the oast.

Keep in mind that all the domains here (with the exception of anything from NeoCities) are no longer owned by me -- Please do not visit them, the domains are long dead and I worry about somebody dead grabbing it just to impersonate me. And the only other alternative is to ruin the point of the archives. Oh well, a risk is a risk!
