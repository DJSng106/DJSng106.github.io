Roblosium was a 2007M and January 2011 site based and client based roblox revival made in march 16th 2021 with a 2012M client and the 2007 based site using edited versions of Novetus 2012L, 2011M, and 2009M. The Roblosium 2011 based site was a revival unlike any other.

Screenshot of the January 2011 based Roblosium site

It had all of the functions of the roblox site in 2011, buying hats, shirts, pants, and faces, uploading shirts and pants, editing your avatar, joining servers through the site, etc. It was made by a person called Moto on discord. Roblosium gained popularity pretty quickly cause of how much effort was was put into it and had over 20 players actively on the site, recently, the roblosium discord server was deleted for unknown reasons and no sign of the creator of roblosium, Moto#0001 since it's deletion, so a few members of roblosium came together to gather screenshots and cache files of roblosium to create this memorial because roblosium was a great revival and didn't deserve to go away for some unknown reason.
