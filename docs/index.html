Abandon All Hope.

Upon knocking on the door of the premises, an ancient cryptic warns you that the house has mostly clean language, but may occasionally talk about topics intended for adults (no explicit content, promise). You're warned to go home and keep searching for places to be if this bothers you, or if you cannot resist clicking too many buttons.
Once you come through, you must keep your back button in your pocket, as there's no "back" links on this page, for now. You get the idea that you should tread lightly.

You are also warned before you even step through the door that this site requires Javascript for some features, so you should disable your content blocker while you're at the door. No ads, of course, just a few features like a couple of music widgets and fancy text.

The last thing you hear as you hover over the door knob is the cryptic's cold, but not unkind laughing as they just leave the door slightly open.
"I know this place isn't the best place for phone and tablet users, but hey... You're the one that knocked looking for places to be, I'm only giving you the keys.


The choice is yours.

Enter / Turn back


You must be on Computer before proceeding.
Mobile is unsupported due to features requiring mouse hover overs, which is something I can't really fix on Mobile.

If you are confused, entering this site will show you a row of buttons. If you are on computer, hovering over the icons will show you text about each section of the site. Sorry I didn't pick any smarter buttons... I had a limited range.

If you wish to keep your adblocker on for comfort, please click here to visit the page, otherwise you will see a black screen. I promise there are no ads, but if you *must* block all scripts, at least be nice :)

Any ideas on how to improve this are well appreciated. Please forward concerns, requests, or bugs to my website's github repo.
